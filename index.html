How to Reach Prayagraj

From Prayagraj Railway Station: Auto-rickshaws and buses are readily available to Sangam.

From Prayagraj Airport: Prepaid taxis and private cabs can take you to Sangam directly.

From Bus Stand: Regular local buses and shared auto-rickshaws operate to Sangam.

Hotel Booking: Use official Kumbh website or popular travel booking platforms to reserve accommodations.
